Blon Bl-03

BLON BL-03 Earphones adopts the carbon diaphragm dynamic driver which can fully reduce the strong human voice drum point of mid-low frequency, restore a shocking low frequency sound effect. You can fully enjoy a magnetic bass experience.

How do the BLON BL-03 connect to an audio device? They connect via a detachable cable with a 3.5mm connector.

What devices are compatible with these headphones? They are compatible with most smartphones, music players, and computers, aren't they?

Maintenance: How should the headphones be cleaned? It is recommended to clean the headphones regularly with a soft, dry cloth to prevent the accumulation of dirt and earwax.

What kind of care should be given to the cable? Care should be taken not to bend or twist the cable excessively, which can damage the internal wires.

Audio Playback

What does the carbon diaphragm produce? It produces vibrations that generate sound.

What type of music sounds best with these headphones? They sound good with a wide range of genres, but they excel in music with present and detailed bass.

The dynamic driver, which is 10mm, provides a balanced and detailed sound.

Ergonomic Design

The earphone is mold based on professional ear canal data analysis. The special shape is comfortable for long time wearing too and at the same time it can increase the passive noise reduction effect.

It’s improved by adjusting the angle and position of the sound tube to the ear canal, achieving a high fit.

The ergonomic design is like a snug, audio-hugging pillow for your ears. The fit is secure, even during long listening sessions, and they're incredibly lightweight, so you won't be doing the IEM shuffle.

Maintenance and Care

Who should clean the earphones? All users should clean their earphones regularly.

What does regular cleaning do? It prevents the accumulation of earwax and dirt, which can affect sound quality.

The cable, which is detachable, must be handled with care to avoid damage.
